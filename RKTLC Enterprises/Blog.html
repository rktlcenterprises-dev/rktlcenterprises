Import Export Code (IEC)

IEC is mandatory for businesses in India to import or export goods. It acts as a unique identification for customs and DGFT purposes.

  • Required for all import/export transactions
  • Issued by DGFT after PAN verification
  • Valid for lifetime

How to Apply: Submit PAN, address proof, and bank details via DGFT online portal.

Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG)

EPCG scheme allows businesses to import capital goods at reduced or zero customs duty for manufacturing export products.

  • Reduces upfront investment costs
  • Supports modernization of production units
  • Export obligation must be fulfilled

How to Apply: Apply online via DGFT with export plan and documents.

RoDTEP (Remission of Duties & Taxes on Exported Products)

Refunds certain duties and taxes incurred in manufacturing export products to enhance competitiveness.

  • Reimburses embedded taxes like VAT, fuel, electricity
